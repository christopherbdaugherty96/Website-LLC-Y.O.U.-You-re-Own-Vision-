How I think about websites

A good local website should answer the customer’s first questions fast.

What do you do?

Services should be obvious without digging.

Can I trust you?

Photos, examples, reviews, and clear presentation help the business feel real.

How do I contact you?

Calls, quote buttons, email, and directions should be easy to find.
