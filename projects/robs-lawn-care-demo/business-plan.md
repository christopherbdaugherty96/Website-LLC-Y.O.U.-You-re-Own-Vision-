# Rob’s Lawn Care — Business Foundation

## Business Name
Rob’s Lawn Care

## Owner
Rob

## Location
Ypsilanti, Michigan

## Business Type
Local lawn care and property maintenance service.

## Mission Statement
At Rob’s Lawn Care, our mission is to provide dependable, affordable, and high-quality lawn maintenance services that help homeowners and local property owners keep their yards clean, sharp, and well maintained. We take pride in honest work, consistent results, and reliable communication for customers in Ypsilanti, Michigan and nearby communities.

## Business Overview
Rob’s Lawn Care is a locally operated lawn care business based out of Ypsilanti, Michigan. The business is built around simple, practical outdoor services that customers need regularly: mowing, trimming, and yard cleanup. Rob’s Lawn Care is positioned as a dependable neighborhood service for homeowners, renters, landlords, and small property owners who want their yards maintained without hassle.

The website should focus on bringing in inquiries, making it easy for customers to request service, and presenting Rob as local, reliable, and easy to contact.

## Core Services

### Lawn Mowing
Routine lawn mowing for residential and small property customers. Service should emphasize clean cuts, consistent scheduling, and dependable results.

### Weed Whacking / Trimming
Trimming around fences, sidewalks, driveways, trees, flower beds, and hard-to-reach areas so the whole yard looks finished.

### Debris Removal
Removal of light yard debris such as branches, leaves, grass clippings, and general outdoor clutter to help leave the property cleaner and more presentable.

## Target Customers
- Homeowners in Ypsilanti and surrounding areas
- Renters responsible for yard upkeep
- Landlords with small residential properties
- Older customers who need help maintaining their yard
- Busy families who want reliable lawn service
- Small local property owners needing seasonal cleanup

## Website Goal
The main goal of the Rob’s Lawn Care website is to generate inquiries. The site should make it easy for a visitor to:

1. Understand what services are offered
2. Trust that Rob is local and reliable
3. Call, text, or submit an inquiry form
4. Request a quote for mowing, trimming, or debris removal
