# Bryan's Neighborhood Coney — Third Pass Polish Plan

## Goal
Turn the Bryan's demo from a basic restaurant concept into a pitch-ready local business website demo that feels polished, trustworthy, and conversion-focused.

## Refined Positioning
Bryan's should be presented as a dependable Waterford breakfast-and-lunch neighborhood favorite: fast, friendly, family-style, and easy to return to.

## Refined Headline Options
1. Waterford's Neighborhood Favorite for Breakfast & Coney Classics
2. Breakfast, Coneys, and Diner Favorites Served Daily in Waterford
3. Your Local Waterford Stop for Breakfast, Burgers, and Coney Classics

## Refined Supporting Copy
Start your morning with classic breakfast plates, omelettes, hash favorites, coney dogs, and comforting diner staples served daily in Waterford.

## Homepage Structure (Polished)
1. Hero section
   - Headline
   - Supporting copy
   - Primary CTA: Call Now
   - Secondary CTA: Get Directions
2. Trust bar
   - Daily hours
   - Waterford location
   - Fast breakfast and lunch service
3. Breakfast favorites section
   - Bryan's Special
   - Eggs Benedict
   - Kentucky Hash and Eggs
   - French Toast
4. Diner classics section
   - Coney Dogs
   - Patty Melt
   - Wet Burrito
   - Greek Salad
5. Why locals come back
   - Friendly service
   - Big breakfast variety
   - Easy stop for breakfast or lunch
6. Reviews block
   - Short review snippets or placeholders
7. Photo strip
   - Breakfast plates
   - Omelettes
   - Coney dogs
   - Diner interior
8. Map and contact section
   - Address
   - Phone
   - Hours
9. Sticky mobile CTA bar
   - Call
   - Directions

## Menu Presentation Strategy
Do not show an overwhelming full diner wall of text first.
Instead:
- Start with featured breakfast favorites
- Then use category cards
- Then optionally add a longer menu section lower on the page

Recommended category cards:
- Breakfast Specials
- Omelettes
- Pancakes & French Toast
- Coneys & Hot Dogs
- Burgers & Sandwiches
- Salads & Sides

## Visual Design Direction
- Warm diner palette: cream, red, charcoal, muted gold accents
- Rounded cards and large readable buttons
- Friendly typography, not luxury typography
- Bright breakfast imagery near the top
- Simple, clean sections with strong spacing

## Conversion Priorities
1. Phone number visible above the fold
2. Hours visible without scrolling far
3. Directions button always available
4. Menu highlights visible fast
5. Mobile layout designed first

## Owner Pitch Angle
This is not a fancy restaurant redesign pitch.
It is a practical local-business growth pitch:
- easier for customers to find you
- easier to call
- easier to check hours
- easier to pick a meal fast

## Suggested Outreach Framing
I put together a polished mobile-friendly website concept for Bryan's Neighborhood Coney that highlights your breakfast favorites, daily hours, location, and quick call-to-action flow. I’d love to show you what it could look like.
