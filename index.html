FAQ

Simple answers before we talk.

What kind of businesses do you build for?

Local restaurants, lawn care companies, contractors, service businesses, personal brands, and owners who need a cleaner online presence.

Do I need to know exactly what I want?

No. A basic goal, business type, and a few examples are enough to start shaping the site.

Can you redesign an existing website?

Yes. A refresh can keep the core idea while improving trust, layout, mobile usability, and customer action paths.

How do customers contact me from the site?

The site can include call buttons, email buttons, inquiry forms, directions, booking links, and service request sections.
