## What Auralis Digital Does
Auralis Digital builds clean, practical, mobile-ready websites for local businesses that need a stronger online presence.

The focus is simple:
- make the business look credible online
- explain services clearly
- make it easier for customers to call, book, visit, or request a quote
- improve first impressions
- turn online visitors into real opportunities

## Current Website State
The main homepage currently includes:
- premium dark visual style
- robust SVG logo asset
- clear hero offer
- pricing section
- services section
- sample work section
- founder-led trust positioning
- FAQ section
- Michigan service area section
- lead intake form that prepares a project email
- mobile CTA and desktop sticky contact bar
- SEO metadata and local business structured data
- accessibility improvements such as skip link, focus states, labels, and autocomplete fields

## Important Current Gap
The contact form currently opens a prepared email. It is useful, but it is not yet a true backend lead form.

Recommended next upgrade:
- Formspree, Netlify Forms, Basin, Google Forms, or a custom backend endpoint
- automatic email notification
- optional lead spreadsheet or CRM tracking
