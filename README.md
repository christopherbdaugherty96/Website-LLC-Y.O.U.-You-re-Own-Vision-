## Delivery Workflow

Standard flow:

1. Collect intake.
2. Confirm goal, scope, budget, assets, and timeline.
3. Prepare quote and scope.
4. Build or refresh the website.
5. Review revisions inside agreed scope.
6. Test mobile, links, forms, domain, and hosting.
7. Launch or hand off.
8. Offer optional $250/month website retainer if the site is a good fit.

## Ownership and Hosting Policy

Default recommendation:

- Client owns and pays for their domain.
- Client owns and pays for hosting unless included in a written care plan.
- Auralis may help configure, connect, and maintain the site.
- Any Auralis-managed hosting, billing, or website possession must be documented clearly in writing.
