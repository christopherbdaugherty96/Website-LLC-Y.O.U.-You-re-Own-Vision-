# Client Proof Workflow

Last updated: 2026-04-26

## Purpose

Turn real completed client work into usable proof assets.

No fake proof. No invented metrics. No testimonials without approval.

---

# Step 1 — Finish Strong

Before asking for anything:

- Confirm the client is happy
- Fix obvious bugs
- Confirm live links work
- Confirm mobile view is acceptable
- Confirm contact path works

---

# Step 2 — Ask for Feedback

Simple ask:

"How are you feeling about the finished site? Anything you'd like adjusted?"

Listen first.

---

# Step 3 — Request Testimonial

If positive:

"I'm glad you’re happy with it. Would you be open to a short testimonial I can use on my site?"

---

# Step 4 — Request Portfolio Permission

"Would you be okay with me showing the project in my portfolio as an example of my work?"

---

# Step 5 — Capture Assets

Collect:

- Final screenshots
- Live URL
n- Approved testimonial
- Client name/business name
- Industry/location
- What problem was solved

---

# Step 6 — Publish Carefully

Use:

- `docs/CASE_STUDY_TEMPLATE.md`
- Homepage proof section
- Portfolio/demo area
- Future sales material

---

# Step 7 — Ask for Referral

If they are happy:

"Do you know any other local businesses that could use help with their website?"

---

# Rule

One real client can create:

- testimonial
- case study
- screenshots
- referral
- confidence
- pricing proof
- better future close rate
