# Objection Handling

## Too Expensive
I understand. The goal is not just a website, it is helping customers trust you and contact you more easily. One new customer can often justify the investment.

## Not Right Now
No problem. When would be a better time to revisit it? I can also show you a simple starter option.

## We Already Have a Website
That makes sense. Many businesses keep their current site and still improve design, mobile usability, or conversion paths.

## Need to Think About It
Absolutely. What part would you like to think through most: price, timing, or value?
