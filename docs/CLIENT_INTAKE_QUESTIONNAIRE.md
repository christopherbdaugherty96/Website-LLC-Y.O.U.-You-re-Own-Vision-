# Client Intake Questionnaire

Last updated: 2026-04-26

## Purpose

Use this after a lead becomes interested and before quoting or building. The goal is to collect enough information to scope the project clearly without overcomplicating the first sale.

---

# Business Basics

- Business name:
- Contact name:
- Email:
- Phone:
- City/service area:
- Current website:
- Current social links:

---

# What They Need

- Do you need a brand new website or a refresh of an existing one?
- What is the main reason you want to improve your website now?
- What do you want customers to do most?
  - Call
  - Request a quote
  - Book
  - Visit
  - Email
  - View menu/services
- What services or products should be highlighted first?

---

# Trust / Content

- Do you have a logo?
- Do you have photos?
- Do you have customer reviews/testimonials we can use?
- Do you have before/after photos or project examples?
- What makes your business different from nearby competitors?

# Practical Details

- Preferred launch timeline:
- Budget range:
- Who will approve the final site?
- Any websites you like?
- Any websites you dislike?
- Any required colors, fonts, or brand preferences?

---

# Red Flags / Scope Notes

Clarify early if they ask for:

- online payments
- booking systems
- customer accounts
- custom apps
- e-commerce
- complex automations
- ongoing content writing
- advanced SEO guarantees

These may require a custom quote or later phase.

---

# Closing Question

"If we could fix one thing about your online presence first, what would matter most?"
