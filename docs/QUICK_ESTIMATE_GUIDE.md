# Quick Estimate Guide

Last updated: 2026-04-26

## Purpose
nUse this to give fast, grounded starting ranges during early conversations without promising a final quote before scope is clear.

Final pricing depends on scope, content readiness, timeline, and requested features.

---

# Core Packages

## Website Refresh

Good for outdated sites needing clearer messaging, trust, and easier contact paths.

Typical range:
- $250 to $600+

## Basic Website

Good for businesses needing a simple professional presence.

Typical range:
- $500 to $1,000+

## Standard Website

Good for businesses needing stronger presentation and more pages/sections.

Typical range:
- $1,000 to $2,000+

## Custom / Premium

Good for businesses needing special features or larger scope.

Typical range:
- Custom quote

---

# Price Increases Usually Come From

- More pages
- Copywriting needed
- Photo cleanup/content creation
- Rush timeline
- Booking/forms/integrations
- Large revisions beyond agreed scope
- Ongoing maintenance requests

---

# Good Sales Language

"Based on what you described, you’re likely in the [range] depending on final scope. I’d confirm the exact quote after a quick review of your needs."

---

# Avoid

- Guaranteeing a final price too early
- Underpricing difficult work to win the deal
- Promising advanced features inside a basic package
- Confusing the client with too many options
