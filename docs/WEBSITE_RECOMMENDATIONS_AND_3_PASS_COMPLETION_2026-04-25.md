# Auralis Digital Website Recommendations and 3-Pass Completion

Date: 2026-04-25  
Domain: https://www.auralisdigital.net/  
Repo: `christopherbdaugherty96/Auralis-Digital`

## Goal
Improve the Auralis Digital website so it is clearer, more trustworthy, more conversion-focused, and ready to support future Nova services without confusing the current web design offer.

## Current Positioning
Auralis Digital is a founder-led digital services business serving Michigan small businesses, especially local service businesses that need a stronger online presence.

The legal/business direction should remain broad enough for future services:

- Website design
- Website refreshes
- Local business digital presence
- Automation consulting
- Future Nova-powered AI services

Recommended public brand: **Auralis Digital**  
Recommended legal direction: **Auralis Digital LLC** or another broad LLC name, not a narrow “web design only” entity.

## Key Recommendations

### 1. Strengthen the hero message
The first screen should clearly answer:

- What does Auralis Digital do?
- Who is it for?
- Why should someone trust it?
- What should they do next?

Recommended hero direction:

> Websites that help local businesses look professional, earn trust, and get more calls.

### 2. Make conversion paths obvious
The site should repeatedly offer direct next steps:

- Start a Website Project
- Get a Quote
- Call Now
- Prepare Project Email

These should appear in the hero, service sections, pricing section, contact section, sticky CTA, and mobile CTA.

### 3. Add practical trust signals
The site should continue emphasizing:

- Founder-led service
- Belleville / Michigan local positioning
- Direct communication
- Mobile-first design
- Clear pricing starting points
- Example work
- Plain-language process

### 4. Keep claims grounded
Avoid overstating client results until real testimonials and analytics exist. Use language like:

- “Designed to help customers call, book, visit, or request a quote.”
- “Example builds.”
- “Starter pricing.”
- “Future Nova-powered services.”

### 5. Prepare for Nova without diluting current sales
Nova should be presented as a future-ready service lane, not the main offer yet.

Recommended section:

**Future-ready: Nova-powered business tools**

Possible future services:

- AI website assistants
- Lead intake helpers
- Appointment request workflows
- Customer FAQ automation
- Business operations support

Current boundary:

> Nova services are planned as future add-ons. The current core offer remains practical website design and digital presence support.

### 6. Improve contact flow
The current mailto form is acceptable for a starter site, but the next real upgrade should be a true backend form.

Recommended options:

- Formspree
- Netlify Forms
- Basin
- Google Forms
- Custom backend endpoint

A real form should support:

- Email notification
- Lead storage
- Spam protection
- Optional CRM or spreadsheet tracking

### 7. SEO and local pages
The homepage already includes useful SEO basics. Continue building:

- Belleville web design page
- Ypsilanti web design page
- Ann Arbor web design page
- Detroit Metro web design page
- Sitemap and internal links
- Google Search Console verification
- Google Business Profile once business details are ready
