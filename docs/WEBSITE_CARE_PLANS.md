# Website Care Plans

Status: operating policy / service template

Auralis Digital may offer optional monthly website care plans for clients who want ongoing support after launch.

The purpose of a care plan is to keep the website current, useful, and accurate without requiring the client to make every update themselves.

---

## Default Ownership Rule

By default, the client should own and pay for their own domain and hosting accounts.

Auralis Digital can help choose, connect, configure, and maintain those services, but domain ownership should remain in the client's name whenever possible.

This protects both sides:

- the client keeps control of their business identity
- Auralis is not stuck paying client renewals unexpectedly
- access and handoff stay cleaner
- cancellation is less confusing

---

## Optional Managed Website Arrangement

If a client wants Auralis Digital to manage or hold possession of the website setup, that must be documented in writing before launch.

The agreement should clearly state:

- who owns the domain
- who pays domain renewal costs
- who owns the hosting account
- who pays hosting costs
- who owns the website files/content
- what is included in the monthly fee
- what happens if the client cancels
- how transfer or handoff works
- what happens if payment is late or missed

Do not casually hold a client's domain or website without written terms.

## Example Care Plan Tiers

### Basic Care

Suggested range: $50/month

Best for simple websites that only need occasional updates.

Possible inclusions:

- small text updates
- phone/email/hour changes
- link checks
- minor photo swaps
- basic monthly review

### Growth Care

Suggested range: $100-$150/month

Best for businesses that run specials, seasonal updates, menu/service changes, or regular promotions.

Possible inclusions:

- monthly specials updates
- service/menu updates
- new photos or gallery swaps
- basic SEO/content edits
- contact-flow checks
- priority response over non-retainer clients

### Managed Website Plan

Suggested range: $200+/month

Best for clients who want Auralis Digital to actively maintain the site and coordinate hosting/domain details.

Possible inclusions:

- hosting coordination
- frequent website updates
- lead-flow checks
- small landing page or seasonal section updates
- content refreshes
- priority support
- basic performance and mobile checks

---

## Update Limits

Avoid promising unlimited updates unless the plan truly supports it.

Recommended language:

> Includes up to a reasonable number of small update requests per month. Larger redesigns, new pages, custom integrations, e-commerce, booking systems, or major copy rewrites are quoted separately.

Examples of small updates:

- change business hours
- update phone/email
- post a monthly special
- change pricing text
- swap a few photos
- update a service description

Examples of larger work:

- full redesign
- new multi-section page
- booking system setup
- online payments
- e-commerce
- advanced automation
- large gallery rebuild

---

## Cancellation and Handoff

A care plan should state what happens if the client cancels.

Recommended default:

- client keeps ownership of their domain if it is in their account
- client keeps access to hosting if it is in their account
- Auralis removes itself from ongoing support obligations after final handoff
- unpaid work is not performed after cancellation
- transfer help may be billed separately if not included

If Auralis owns or controls hosting on behalf of the client, the cancellation terms must explain how the site can be transferred, exported, paused, or taken down.

---

## Internal Rule

Auralis Digital can sell maintenance, updates, and managed support.

Do not blur the difference between:

1. building the website
2. hosting the website
3. owning the domain
4. updating the website
5. managing the client relationship

Each one should be clear in the quote or care plan.
